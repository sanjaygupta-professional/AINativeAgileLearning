# Chapter 9: Fat Tony and the Fragilistas

> "Fat Tony has an attribute: he doesn't believe in explanations."

## The Core Insight

Taleb introduces **Fat Tony**, a Brooklyn character who represents practical wisdom‚Äîthe ability to thrive through street smarts rather than academic knowledge. His opposite is the **Fragilista**: someone who applies fragile theoretical frameworks to complex situations.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Practical wisdom matters** ‚Äî Street smarts often beat book smarts
2. **Models aren't reality** ‚Äî The map is not the territory
3. **Skin in the game filters** ‚Äî Trust those with something to lose
4. **Context matters** ‚Äî Abstract answers can be practically wrong
