# Via Negativa

**Via Negativa** is the principle that you gain more by removing than by adding. Subtraction is often more powerful, predictable, and robust than addition.

## Concept Overview

```mermaid
mindmap
  root((Via Negativa))
    Definition
      Gain by removing
      Subtract harms
      Less is more
    Applications
      Health: Remove toxins
      Wealth: Avoid losses
      Knowledge: Know what NOT to do
    Why It Works
      Harm is clear
      Removal is predictable
      Addition has unknowns
```
